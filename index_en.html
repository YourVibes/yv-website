Talking about us

YourVibes

YourVibes is the app that simplifies the creation and participation in events, making them accessible to everyone.
With YourVibes you can create events by sharing them with the community, participate in events created by other users and discover new emotions.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To make it easier to meet, connect and create authentic social relationships.

Vision: To become one of the leading tools globally in the management and research sector of events.

Team

The YourVibes team is made up of a group of passionate and motivated young people, all born in 2004. Together, they have designed and developed the YourVibes app, which allows users to create and participate in events in a simple and intuitive way.

The project was born in the classroom, where the creators encountered the difficulty of organizing events with friends and acquaintances. With YourVibes, they want to make this process easier and more accessible to everyone.

The YourVibes team is constantly working to improve and expand the app's features, to offer an ever more complete and satisfying. With a strong passion for technology and innovation, the YourVibes team is ready to grow their startup and reach new heights.
